ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Founded in the heart of Koukaki, Athens, Urban Vibes was born from a passion for bringing authentic street food culture to the city's vibrant nightlife scene.

Our journey began when chef Alex and mixologist Maria decided to combine their love for bold flavors with the energy of urban living. What started as a small food truck has evolved into one of Athens' most beloved culinary destinations.

We source our ingredients locally, craft our cocktails with precision, and create an atmosphere where friends gather, music plays, and unforgettable moments happen every night.
